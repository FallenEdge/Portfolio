Blackjack Rules:

1. 1 Player plays at a time versus the dealer (CPU)

2. Consists of a single deck of 52 cards

3. Player can place bets in increments of $5

4. Bets can be taken once after each card is drawn

5. Player gets two cards on the initial deal

6. Dealer gets one card on the initial deal

7. Each turn, player can choose to "hit" (take a card), "stand" (end their turn without taking a card), "double" (double the wager, take a card, and end their turn), "surrender" (half their bet and retire from the game)

8. Picture cards count as 10 and aces are either 1 or 11 depending on the player's choice

9. If the total cards exceeds 21, then it busts

10. Dealer stands at 17 or higher, unless hitting 21 before

11. If the player has a higher total, then they win otherwise they lose.
